PHOTO EDITOR — LOCAL TOOL

Add your photos to the portfolio

Upload a photo for each slot below. Each one gets cropped to preview, renamed automatically to the exact filename your website expects, and is ready to download. Nothing uploads anywhere else — everything happens in your browser.

STEP 1

Click Choose photo on each slot below and pick an image from your computer.

STEP 2

Click Download — it saves with the correct filename, ready to use.

STEP 3

Drag the downloaded file into the photos folder of your GitHub repo, replacing nothing else.

Filenames are fixed on purpose — your site looks for exact names like photos/portrait.jpg. Don't rename the downloaded files.
